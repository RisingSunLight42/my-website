Announcement 1 : Book

Preface

As you probably already know, I've been a writer at heart from the beginning, even before I was someone who loved lore, roleplaying, or anything else. Soutsu's story has been around for quite some time now (he's a pretty old character, after all, as of March 3, 2019). Nothing can really be found on the internet anymore, and I even lost the original files due to some superb corruption, but I've already written a lot in the past.

Everything was in Soutsu's original universe by the way :3

So it was obvious that I would eventually pick up the pen I'd abandoned so long ago. This is something that's quite difficult for me. I haven't written seriously for well over a year now, and all the drafts/well-advanced projects have disappeared from my disks with the infamous corruption. Although I have a very good memory, rewriting something identically is impossible, and this has frustrated me deeply.

It's difficult for many reasons. Among them is burnout. You may have noticed, but I disappeared from streaming a few months ago, a lot of things I was more or less teasing never happened, and overall, everything stopped (yes, streaming was supposed to resume the week of May 26, 2025, but my internet connection decided otherwise). I refused to admit for a long time that I was in a state of burnout; in fact, I think I really admitted it to myself once I came out of it. I posted a thread talking about it on Twitter (see here), and I can assure you, I'm feeling much better.

This time I will be careful, I will take the time I need for each project.

Anyway, I've said it all, let's get to the main topic.

Soutsu's Origins, vol 1 : Black and White

Where the infinity of universes, multiverses, and more lie, lay a still-young tower. Barely created, it had only existed for a short time, but the anomaly it represented was already more than enough to intrigue a most singular creature. One desperately sought the one who could paint many worlds within its floors; the other sought knowledge and power. It was from these wishes that the contract was born.

It was from these wishes that the future Soutsu Nareta was born.
